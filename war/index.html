NeuroML Connectome

We captured the positions of the cells in context with each other. We are now building a description of the synaptic junctions and the ion channels for each cell.

Explore the connectome

A modular simulation engine.

We are engineering Geppetto, an open-source modular platform to enable multi-scale and multi-algorithm simulation of biological systems.
The interface? Your browser.

OpenWorm Browser
